My wife Cat and I have talked for years about buying a boat big enough to live aboard full time. We looked at dozens of boats over the last couple of years. We saw a few we liked, but the circumstances were never quite right.

Then, in December 2015, we found "Rafiki." This time, circumstances were right: on a month-to-month lease, we have the money, etc. You can't talk for years about doing something, then not do it when you can, right? Plus, the boat is awesome!

She's a classic 42-foot trawler built 1962 in Seattle, WA. Wooden hull, single (Grey Marine) diesel engine. She's been beautifully restored by the previous owners. Aft master cabin with queen bed and 3/4 bath. Adorable galley and salon amidship. Up front is a V-berth with 2 twin beds and 1/2 bath.

I created this website to document (and share) our journey.
